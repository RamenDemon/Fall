Pumpkin Carvings: A Fall Tradition

The tradition of carving pumpkins known as Jack-o'-Lanterns was brought to America by the Irish. The Irish would carve large turnips and potatoes and place lights in them to scare away the legend, Stingy Jack, and other evil spirits. This all began after the story a man known as Stingy Jack.
